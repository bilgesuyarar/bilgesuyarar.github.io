Sudoku Matrix – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku Matrix (“Application”, “App”) handles user data. The App is developed and published by Bilgesu Yarar (“Developer”).

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Sudoku Matrix is designed to respect your privacy. The App does not collect or process any personally identifiable information (PII).

The only data stored is non-personal gameplay information saved locally on your device:

  • Puzzle progress
  • Notes, mistakes, timer, difficulty
  • User preferences (sound, vibration, theme, etc.)

This information is stored using SharedPreferences and never leaves your device. The developer does not have access to this data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal identifiers such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (non-personal)
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)

AdMob processes data under Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No other third-party analytics, tracking, or SDKs are used.

3. How Your Data Is Used

The App uses data only for the following purposes:

  • Saving your game progress
  • Enabling local game features (notes, mistakes, settings, etc.)
  • Enabling ads to function properly

The App does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Create user profiles
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Sell or share any user information

4. Data Storage & Retention

All gameplay data is stored only on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Deleting the App, or
  • Clearing the App’s storage from device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Matrix is designed for a general audience and does not collect any personal information. Because no personal data is collected, the App is suitable for younger users as well.

6. Security

Although the App does not process sensitive information, all locally stored data is protected by your device’s built-in security mechanisms (Android/iOS sandboxing).

No data is transmitted online.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be posted on the App Store and Google Play listing pages.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
